Mersedes

The Mercedes brand turns 120 in 2022. Today, the Mercedes-Benz automobile brand is faithful to tradition, an example of power, and an advanced position in the automotive industry, just like in the first year of its management.


BMW

The history of BMW began in the Bavarian Munich, where in 1913 the German industrialists Rapp and Otto opened two small enterprises. The new firms were engaged in the production of aircraft engines, and their leaders soon decided to unite in order to withstand tough market competition together.


Mazda

January 30 is the birthday of Mazda from Hiroshima. In 1920, its founder, Zujiro Matsuda (1875-1952), managed to acquire a bankrupt company, which turned out to be the forerunner of the famous Japanese automaker.

Audi

The first car of the Wanderer brand appeared in 1913, August Horch organized his first own enterprise (Horch & Co.) in 1899, and after creditors removed him from business, Audi Automobil-Werke was organized in 1909, which translates as "Audi Automobile Factory".
